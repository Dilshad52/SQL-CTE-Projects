# SQL-CTE-Project
# HR Salary Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee salary and revenue data using SQL, Power BI, and business analytics techniques.

The objective is to identify salary trends, department performance, top earners, and revenue contribution across departments to support HR and management decision-making.

---

## Skills Demonstrated

* SQL CTEs (Common Table Expressions)
* Advanced Subqueries
* Correlated Subqueries
* Salary Ranking Analysis
* Department-wise Performance Analysis
* Business Intelligence Reporting
* Data Visualization with Power BI

---

## Business Questions Solved

1. Which department has the highest average salary?
2. Which employees earn above their department average?
3. Who are the highest-paid employees?
4. Which department generates the most revenue?
5. How is payroll distributed across departments?

---

## Dashboard KPIs

* Total Employees
* Total Payroll
* Average Salary
* Highest Salary
* Total Revenue

---

## Key Insights

* Finance department has the highest average salary.
* Finance also contributes the highest revenue.
* Most high-salary employees belong to Finance and IT departments.
* Several employees earn above their department average, indicating strong performance.
* Revenue generation is concentrated in technical and finance roles.
